Our Services

With extensive expertise in hydroelectric power plant operations, we offer specialized O&M services ensuring efficiency, reliability, and longevity of hydropower assets.

Routine & Preventive Maintenance

Regular servicing to prevent breakdowns and optimize performance. Scheduled inspections and proactive maintenance to extend asset life.

Corrective Maintenance & Troubleshooting

Fast and effective solutions to technical failures. Expert diagnosis and repair to restore plant operations quickly and reliably.

Performance Monitoring & Optimization

Utilizing advanced tools and analytics to enhance plant efficiency. Data-driven insights to maximize generation output.

Emergency Response & Breakdown Support

Rapid assistance to minimize downtime and operational disruptions. 24/7 emergency response for critical situations.

Spare Parts Management & Procurement

Ensuring a seamless supply chain for critical plant components. Strategic inventory management to prevent costly delays.

Training & Capacity Building

Technical training to enhance in-house maintenance capabilities. Upskilling teams for sustainable, long-term plant operations.

Case Project

Project TH — Telekosang Hydro

When tasked with revamping the Telekosang Hydro O&M team, we took strategic actions that led to a full transformation of operations.

  • Conducted a full operational audit to identify gaps and inefficiencies
  • Restructured team roles, SOPs, and implemented performance tracking
  • Recruited skilled personnel and introduced targeted training programs
  • Cleared maintenance backlogs and established a preventive maintenance regime
  • Introduced data-driven monitoring and root cause analysis
  • Streamlined spare parts management and emergency response procedures

These efforts turned the team into a professional, high-performing unit. This success inspired the formation of EOM, focused on delivering world-class O&M services for hydropower plants.
